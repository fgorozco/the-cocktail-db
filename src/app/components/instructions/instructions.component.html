Instructions

  • 1. In a small mixer bowl beat egg yolks till blended.
  • 2. Gradually add 1/4 cup sugar, beating at high speed till thick and lemon colored.
  • 3. Stir in milk, stir in rum, bourbon, vanilla, and salt.
  • 4. Chill thoroughly. Whip cream. Wash beaters well.
  • 5. In a large mixer bowl beat egg whites till soft peaks form.
  • 6. Gradually add remaining 1/4 cup sugar, beating to stiff peaks.
  • 7. Sprinkle nutmeg over each serving.
  • 8. Serve in a punch bowl or another big bowl.

NOTE: For a nonalcoholic eggnog, prepare Eggnog as above, except omit the bourbon and rum and increase the milk to 3 cups.
